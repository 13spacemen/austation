Zams, Ashlynn updated:

  • a boatload new viro symptoms, check the wiki for more in-depth explanations of each
  • Autobrewery syndrome. Gets the host drunk. has thresholds to change how drunk it gets the host.
  • Bubonic infection. The host takes toxin and brute damage as they grow pustules that can shoot off and infect others, especially when hit
  • Photosensitive muscle condensation. The host gains more max health and is slowed down in the light, and loses max health and is sped up in the dark
  • Hemetophagy. The host sucks up blood on the floor and that they have drank to cure bloodloss, but loses blood over time. At thresholds, can heal brute damage.
  • Hyperactivity. Lowers mood and reduces nutrition, but heals stamina damage
  • Aptotic Culling. Converts radiation to brute damage, can convert cell and toxin damage at thresholds
  • Organ regeneration. Heals organ damage. At thresholds, prevents and cures heart attacks and appendicitis, and can slowly regenerate organs the host is missing
  • Viral Suspended Animation. Causes a virus to have next to no effect until reaching stage 5
  • Hysteria. Causes a random emote, which does not change when the virus transmits. Cannot cause farting. At threshold, infects whoever is looking and is not mindshielded or wearing a tinfoil hat
  • Toxoplasmosis Sapiens. Grants a mood boost around cats and cat people. Can cause some forms of dysmorphic mania at thresholds
  • a new, better, more evil adminspawn toxoplasmosis virus preset
  • Xenobiological Symbiosis. Heals toxin damage. The host barfs out grubs that eat slimes and slimepeople, but can be cooked into delicious healing meat
  • new sprites for grubs
  • Gravitic Biopotential Instability Syndrome, or GBIS. Causes random gravity increases, with thresholds can deal damage or throw objects at the host
  • removes high gravity animations. They're broken, and cause you to become invisible.
